**Untappd**

## 🇨🇦 Canada (14)

### Brasserie du Bas-Canada (2019) — Gatineau, QC {.brewery}

A craft brewery founded in 2019 in Gatineau, Quebec. Specialising in generously hopped IPAs and DIPAs, it is renowned for its juicy, tropical and intensely aromatic beers. One of the best breweries in Canada.

- **Website:** [https://www.brasseriebascanada.com](https://www.brasseriebascanada.com)

---

### Messorem (2017) — Montréal, QC {.brewery}

Founded in 2017 in Montréal, Messorem Bracitorium (Latin for "the reaper") is a cult craft brewery specialising in IPAs, DIPAs and TIPAs. Known for its Temporalis series and distinctive can art, it is an essential name on the Quebec craft scene.

- **Website:** [https://www.messorem.co](https://www.messorem.co)

---

### Third Moon Brewing Company (2018) — Milton, ON {.brewery}

Founded in 2018 in Milton, Ontario, Third Moon is renowned for its hazy IPAs and rich stouts. A limited-distribution brewery, its releases often sell out within minutes.

- **Website:** [https://thirdmoonbrewing.com](https://thirdmoonbrewing.com)

---

### Badlands Brewing Company (2020) — Caledon, ON {.brewery}

Founded in 2020 in Caledon, Ontario, Badlands stands out with its creative IPAs and sours. Inspired by the wild landscapes of the region, it offers bold and well-hopped beers.

- **Website:** [https://www.badlandsbrewing.ca](https://www.badlandsbrewing.ca)

---

### Wood Brothers Brewing Co. (2019) — Glen Robertson, ON {.brewery}

Founded in 2019 in Canada, Wood Brothers is a family brewery specialising in pale ales and IPAs. Their fresh, fruity beers have become benchmarks on the Canadian craft scene.

- **Website:** [https://www.woodbrothersbrewingtogo.com](https://www.woodbrothersbrewingtogo.com)

---

### Le Ketch — Sainte-Flavie, QC {.brewery}

A Canadian craft brewery specialising in IPAs and characterful hopped beers. A rising name on the Canadian craft scene.

- **Website:** [https://www.leketch.com](https://www.leketch.com)

---

### Fine Balance Brewing — Kingston, ON {.brewery}

A Canadian brewery that lives up to its name: balanced beers, polished IPAs and mastered flavours. A discreet but formidable hop artisan.

- **Website:** [https://finebalancebrewing.ca](https://finebalancebrewing.ca)

---

### Wills Brewing — Montréal, QC {.brewery}

A Canadian craft brewery offering creative IPAs and pale ales. An artisanal and authentic approach to craft beer.

- **Website:** [https://www.wills.beer](https://www.wills.beer)

---

### Willibald Farm Brewery — Ayr, ON {.brewery}

A Canadian farm brewery set in a bucolic landscape. Willibald offers craft beers brewed with local ingredients, blending farming tradition and brewing creativity.

- **Website:** [https://www.drinkwillibald.com](https://www.drinkwillibald.com)

---

### Bad Bones Beer (2022) — Dunham, QC {.brewery}

Born from a recovery project after a car accident, Bad Bones is a Dunham nanobrewery specialising in ultra-hopped IPAs — NEIPAs, doubles and triples with New Zealand and American hops. A human story as much as a brewing adventure.

- **Website:** [https://www.badbonesbeer.com](https://www.badbonesbeer.com)

---

### Nano Cinco (2021) — Québec, QC {.brewery}

Founded in 2021 in Québec City's Limoilou neighbourhood, Nano Cinco produces bold IPAs and creative stouts in small batches. A craft brewery focused on experimentation and regular releases.

- **Website:** [https://nanocinco.com](https://nanocinco.com)

---

### Noctem Artisans Brasseurs (2015) — Québec, QC {.brewery}

Opened in 2015 in Québec City, Noctem is a brewpub that has become essential on the Quebec craft scene. Known for its IPAs (including the famous Catnip), lagers and fruit beers, it blends tradition with modern trends.

- **Website:** [https://www.noctem.ca](https://www.noctem.ca)

---

### Brewskey (2015) — Montréal, QC {.brewery}

Opened in 2015 in Old Montreal, Brewskey is a brewpub, taproom and annexe brewing on site. Thirty taps, polished food and a terrace facing the port — an essential address on the Montreal craft scene.

- **Website:** [https://www.brewskey.ca](https://www.brewskey.ca)

---

### Sir John Brewing Co. (2022) — Lachute, QC {.brewery}

Founded in 2022 in Lachute, Laurentides, Sir John is an independent brewery inspired by the American craft tradition. Creative IPAs, bourbon barrel-aged stouts and seasonal beers in a welcoming tasting room.

- **Website:** [https://www.brasseriesirjohn.com](https://www.brasseriesirjohn.com)

## 🇺🇸 United States (19)

### The Alchemist (2003) — Stowe, VT {.brewery}

Founded in 2003 in Vermont, The Alchemist is the brewery that popularised the Hazy IPA with its legendary Heady Topper, considered one of the best beers in the world. An institution of American craft beer.

- **Website:** [https://alchemistbeer.com](https://alchemistbeer.com)

---

### Trillium Brewing Company (2013) — Boston, MA {.brewery}

Founded in 2013 in Boston, Trillium is one of the most acclaimed breweries on the East Coast. Its IPAs, DIPAs and farmhouse ales are regularly ranked among the best in the world. A must on the American craft scene.

- **Website:** [https://www.trilliumbrewing.com](https://www.trilliumbrewing.com)

---

### Tree House Brewing Company (2011) — Charlton, MA {.brewery}

Founded in 2011 in Charlton, Massachusetts, Tree House has become a legend of American craft beer. Its Julius IPA is considered one of the best IPAs in the world. A cult brewery with on-site-only distribution.

- **Website:** [https://treehousebrew.com](https://treehousebrew.com)

---

### Monkish Brewing Co. (2012) — Torrance, CA {.brewery}

Founded in 2012 in Torrance, California, Monkish is the benchmark for Hazy IPAs on the West Coast. Originally inspired by Belgian brewing traditions, it evolved towards hazy, juicy IPAs that draw queues at every release.

- **Website:** [https://www.monkishbrewing.com](https://www.monkishbrewing.com)

---

### Mortalis Brewing Company (2018) — Avon, NY {.brewery}

Founded in 2018 in Avon, New York State, Mortalis quickly made a name for itself with its smoothie sours and creative IPAs. Its limited-edition releases create a buzz with every drop.

- **Website:** [https://www.mortalisbrewing.com](https://www.mortalisbrewing.com)

---

### All My Friends Beer Co (2019) — Los Angeles, CA {.brewery}

Founded in 2019 in Los Angeles, All My Friends is a gypsy brewery collaborating with top brewers worldwide. Its tropical IPAs and pastry stouts have become benchmarks on the Californian craft scene.

- **Website:** [https://www.allmyfriendsbeer.com](https://www.allmyfriendsbeer.com)

---

### Lolev Beer (2020) — Pittsburgh, PA {.brewery}

Founded in 2020 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, Lolev Beer is a microbrewery specialising in ultra-hopped IPAs and DIPAs. Still under the radar, it is beginning to make a name on the international scene.

- **Website:** [https://lolev.beer](https://lolev.beer)

---

### BRUJOS (2018) — Portland, OR {.brewery}

Founded in 2018 in Portland, Oregon, BRUJOS is a craft brewery pushing boundaries with bold IPAs, pastry stouts and tropical sours. One of the rising stars of the American craft scene.

- **Website:** [https://www.brujosbrewing.com](https://www.brujosbrewing.com)

---

### Parish Brewing Co. (2003) — Broussard, LA {.brewery}

Founded around 2003 in Louisiana by Andrew Godley, Parish revolutionised the Gulf Coast craft scene with Canebrake, an iconic maple syrup wheat ale. One of the most influential breweries in the American South.

- **Website:** [https://parishbeer.com](https://parishbeer.com)

---

### Finback Brewery (2011) — Queens, NY {.brewery}

Founded in 2011 in Glendale, Queens, Finback is renowned for its hoppy IPAs, sours and barrel-aged beers. A creative New York brewery with a tasting room and regular can releases.

- **Website:** [https://finbackbrewery.com](https://finbackbrewery.com)

---

### Bissell Brothers (2013) — Portland, ME {.brewery}

Launched in 2013 in Portland, Maine, Bissell Brothers made its mark with The Substance Ale and a range of bold IPAs. A brother-owned brewery that introduced modern styles to a region still new to craft beer.

- **Website:** [https://www.bissellbrothers.com](https://www.bissellbrothers.com)

---

### Other Half Brewing (2014) — Brooklyn, NY {.brewery}

Founded in 2014 in Brooklyn, Other Half has become a global benchmark for hazy IPAs and collaborations. Its ultra-hopped beers and limited releases make it one of the most sought-after breweries in the United States.

- **Website:** [https://otherhalfbrewing.com](https://otherhalfbrewing.com)

---

### Deep Fried Beers — Athens, NY {.brewery}

A New York State brewery known for hop-saturated IPAs and bold sours. Associated with Night School in Athens, it offers "decadent" beers that push style boundaries.

- **Website:** [https://www.deepfriedbeers.com](https://www.deepfriedbeers.com)

---

### Fidens Brewing Co. (2019) — Colonie, NY {.brewery}

Founded in 2019 near Albany, New York State, Fidens ("courageous" in Latin) has specialised in juicy, hazy IPAs through a meticulous approach to brewing and dry-hopping.

- **Website:** [https://fidensbrewing.com](https://fidensbrewing.com)

---

### The Veil Brewing Co. (2016) — Richmond, VA {.brewery}

Opened in 2016 in Richmond's Scott's Addition neighbourhood, The Veil made its name with hoppy IPAs and bold sours. One of the most influential breweries on the East Coast, housed in a converted former church.

- **Website:** [https://www.theveilbrewing.com](https://www.theveilbrewing.com)

---

### Phase Three Brewing Company (2019) — Lake Zurich, IL {.brewery}

Founded in 2019 in Lake Zurich, Illinois, Phase Three grew from passionate brewers with backgrounds in restaurants and craft production. Polished IPAs, stouts and lagers from a modern 21,000 sq ft facility.

- **Website:** [https://www.phasethreebrewing.com](https://www.phasethreebrewing.com)

---

### Foam Brewers (2016) — Burlington, VT {.brewery}

Opened in 2016 on the Burlington waterfront in Vermont, Foam was founded by former Switchback and Magic Hat brewers. Hazy IPAs, lagers and sours in a stunning lakeside setting on Lake Champlain.

- **Website:** [https://www.foambrewers.com](https://www.foambrewers.com)

---

### Outer Range Brewing Co. (2016) — Frisco, CO {.brewery}

Founded in late 2016 in Frisco, Colorado, Outer Range draws inspiration from Belgian beers and the Rocky Mountains. IPAs, lagers and mountain beers from an expanded taproom with restaurant and coffee shop in Summit County.

- **Website:** [https://www.outerrangebrewing.com](https://www.outerrangebrewing.com)

---

### Equilibrium Brewery (2016) — Middletown, NY {.brewery}

Commercially brewing since 2016 in Middletown, New York State, Equilibrium applies a scientific approach to brewing for remarkably balanced IPAs and DIPAs. Founded by MIT engineers, it has become a benchmark on the New York craft scene.

- **Website:** [https://www.eqbrew.com](https://www.eqbrew.com)

## 🇧🇪 Belgium (3)

### La Source Beer Co. (2019) — Brussels {.brewery}

Active since 2019 in Brussels, La Source is an artisanal microbrewery and brewpub offering IPAs, sours, wild beers and barrel-aged stouts. Twenty taps at the bar, five connected directly to service tanks for maximum freshness.

- **Website:** [https://lasourcebeer.be](https://lasourcebeer.be)

---

### Misery Beer Co. (2020) — Manoir de Harzé, Ardennes {.brewery}

Nestled in the heart of Liège Ardennes in an authentic renovated manor house, Misery is a family microbrewery and brewpub opened in summer 2020 by Rémy and Samia. Inspired by the North American craft movement and backed by Belgian brewing training, they offer IPAs, pale ales, saison aged in foeders and stouts aged in bourbon or cognac barrels. Beyond their own creations, the manor bar hosts a selection of Belgian and international craft beers, natural wines and light fare based on local Ardennes produce.

- **Website:** [https://miserybeerco.be](https://miserybeerco.be)

---

### Brasserie Surréaliste (2019) — Brussels {.brewery}

Opened in 2019 in Brussels' Dansaert district, Brasserie Surréaliste occupies a 1932 Art Deco building. A brewpub and restaurant offering IPAs, sours and characterful beers in a setting inspired by surrealist art.

- **Website:** [https://www.brasseriesurrealiste.com](https://www.brasseriesurrealiste.com)
