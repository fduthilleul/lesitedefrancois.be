A few handy tips for visiting Paris…

## Transport / Logistics

- The [Citymapper](https://citymapper.com/?lang=fr) app for getting around town.
- Set up a **Navigo pass** on your phone with the [Bonjour RATP](https://www.bonjour-ratp.fr/) app: **add it to Apple Wallet** on iPhone, pay **contactlessly**, and **skip queues** at metro ticket machines.
- **Luggage**: [Bounce](https://bounce.com/fr) or [Nannybag](https://www.nannybag.com/fr). At **Gare du Nord**, be cautious with **left luggage**: there have been **safety incidents** (**bomb alerts** linked to bags have already disrupted or shut down baggage services).
- [Batobus](https://www.batobus.com/fr): **hop-on hop-off**, **nine stops** on the Seine — a **nice alternative to the metro** to see **Paris from the river**.

## Accommodation

- **Prefer Airbnb in Paris**: [Flat near Montmartre](https://fr.airbnb.be/rooms/1252846256859669443), [Flat near Gare du Nord](https://fr.airbnb.be/rooms/25155031), [Another flat near Gare du Nord](https://fr.airbnb.be/rooms/41576801).
- **Alternative**: stay **outside Paris** (often **cheaper**). Example: **Ibis Styles Paris Romainville**, about **300 m** from **metro line 5** into the centre.

## Museums & monuments

- **Louvre** — [guided tour](https://www.ticketlouvre.fr/louvre/b2c/index.cfm/multibuy/events/MEVIND): **book long in advance**.
- **Musée Carnavalet** — [history of Paris](https://www.carnavalet.paris.fr/): a **guided tour** is recommended (thematic tour **“The museum essentials”**) — check the **calendar with filters**.
- **Palais Garnier** — [self-guided tours, guided tours, or immersive game *Arsène Lupin and the opera’s secrets*](https://www.operadeparis.fr/visites/palais-garnier).
- **Hôtel des Invalides** — immersive show [Aura Experience](https://aura-invalides.com/fr_FR/).
- **[BnF](https://www.bnf.fr/fr/la-salle-ovale)** — free, glorious library; **[Galerie Vivienne](https://www.visitparisregion.com/fr/itineraires/galerie-vivienne-i2220233)** is right next door.
- **Musée d’Orsay** — essential; guided tours for families or adults **[the masterpieces](https://www.musee-orsay.fr/fr/agenda/visites-et-ateliers/les-chefs-doeuvre-du-musee-dorsay)** — **book well ahead**.
- **Musée Grévin** — often **long queues**, but **kids love it**. Near [Théâtre des Variétés](https://www.theatredesvarietes.fr/) and [Théâtre des Nouveautés](https://www.theatredesnouveautes.com/).
- **Eiffel Tower** — [tickets](https://ticket.toureiffel.paris/fr), especially **in advance** during **school holidays**; **sparkle** every evening (**about five minutes at the start of each hour**) — since **July 2024**, **Tour Eiffel Effect**, immersive experience on the **first floor**.
- **Sainte-Chapelle** — [advance tickets and commented tours](https://www.sainte-chapelle.fr/visiter/visites-et-activités); best on a **sunny day** for the **stained glass**.
- **Notre-Dame** — **reopened 7 December 2024**; [book online](https://www.notredamedeparis.fr/visiter/reserver/), **mobile app**.
- **Basilica of Sacré-Cœur** — [dome visit](https://www.sacre-coeur-montmartre.com/decouvrir/patrimoine-et-art-sacre/le-dome/) for a **great view**; attending a **Mass** (**four daily**) is a nice way to visit.
- **Eternelle Notre-Dame** — **[virtual tour](https://www.eternellenotredame.com/)**, **beautiful**, **highly recommended**.

## Theatres

- Read **reviews** on [Ticketac](https://www.ticketac.com/).
- *Mon jour de chance* at [Théâtre Fontaine](https://www.theatrefontaine.com/) — **really good**, we **strongly recommend** it.
- *Une idée géniale* — seen in **Brussels** at [Théâtre des Galeries](https://www.theatredesgaleries.be/); in Paris at [Théâtre des Variétés](https://www.theatredesvarietes.fr/) **until 27 April 2025**.
- *ADN* at [Théâtre Michel](https://www.theatre-michel.fr/) — **fun thriller**, gripping **pace**, **sets** and **projected images**.

## Entertainment

- **Le Grand Rex** — [Paris’s largest screen](https://www.legrandrex.com/) with optional **guided visit** or **cinema-themed escape room**; **La Féerie des Eaux** over the **Christmas** period.
- **Outdoor escape games** — [Atlantide](https://www.atlantide.io/) app, *Sous surveillance* mission in the **Marais** from **Place des Vosges** (**free**, geolocation). Organised escapes: **Butte-aux-Cailles** via [My Urban Experience](https://www.myurbanexperience.com/).
- **Seine cruise** — *[La Croisière des Mystères](https://www.vedettesdeparis.fr/fr/ours-cruises/mysteres)* with **[Vedettes de Paris](https://www.vedettesdeparis.fr/)**, embarkation **near the Eiffel Tower**.

- **[Bib Gourmand](https://guide.michelin.com/fr/fr/ile-de-france/paris/restaurants/bib-gourmand)** for **great value** meals.
- **[Cantine De Sam](https://lacantinedesam.site.bykomdab.com/fr)** (near Théâtre Fontaine) or **[Baba](https://babarestaurant.fr)** next door; **[Afendi](https://afendi-paris.com/)** Lebanese near Théâtre Antoine; **[Eats Thyme](https://www.eatsthyme.com/)** Lebanese near the Louvre / Palais-Royal (*Ma Table Libanaise* cookbook); **[Lazzi](https://lazzi.fr/fr/)** before Théâtre Édouard VII (**direct theatre entrance** from the restaurant). For **quirky picks**, try **list-style articles** such as **[Topito](https://www.topito.com/)**.
- **Always double-check** the place still exists and is open when you visit.
