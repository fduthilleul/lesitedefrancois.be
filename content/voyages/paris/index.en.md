A few good tips for visiting Paris…

## Transport / Logistics

- The [Citymapper](https://citymapper.com/?lang=fr) app is perfect for getting around big cities
- You can set up a Navigo card on your phone via the [Bonjour RATP](https://www.bonjour-ratp.fr/) app
- For luggage: [Bounce](https://bounce.com/fr) or [Nannybag](https://www.nannybag.com/fr)
- [Batobus](https://www.batobus.com/fr) hop-on hop-off with 9 stops in the heart of Paris

## Museums & monuments

- **Musée du Louvre** — [guided tour](https://www.ticketlouvre.fr/louvre/b2c/index.cfm/multibuy/events/MEVIND) to book well in advance
- **Palais Garnier** — [Visits](https://www.operadeparis.fr/visites/palais-garnier): self-guided, guided or immersive “Arsène Lupin” game
- **Hôtel des Invalides** — Immersive show [Aura Experience](https://aura-invalides.com/fr_FR/)
- **[Bibliothèque nationale de France](https://www.bnf.fr/fr/la-salle-ovale)** — Free. Stunning library.
- **Musée d’Orsay** — [The Musée d’Orsay masterpieces](https://www.musee-orsay.fr/fr/agenda/visites-et-ateliers/les-chefs-doeuvre-du-musee-dorsay)
- **Musée Grévin** — [Plan your visit](https://www.grevin-paris.com/preparez-votre-visite)
- **Eiffel Tower** — [Buy tickets](https://ticket.toureiffel.paris/fr) in advance
- **Sainte-Chapelle** — [Commented visits](https://www.sainte-chapelle.fr/visiter/visites-et-activites)
- **Notre-Dame de Paris** — Reopened since 7 December 2024, [Booking](https://www.notredamedeparis.fr/visiter/reserver/)
- **Basilique du Sacré-Cœur** — [Dome visit](https://www.sacre-coeur-montmartre.com/decouvrir/patrimoine-et-art-sacre/le-dome/)
- **Eternelle Notre-Dame** — [Virtual tour](https://www.eternellenotredame.com/) of the cathedral

## Theatres

- Read reviews on [Ticketac](https://www.ticketac.com/)
- “Mon jour de chance” at [Théâtre Fontaine](https://www.theatrefontaine.com/)
- “Une idée géniale” at [Théâtre des Variétés](https://www.theatredesvarietes.fr/)
- “ADN” at [Théâtre Michel](https://www.theatre-michel.fr/)

## Entertainment

- **Le Grand Rex** — Paris’s largest screen, cinema-themed escape game
- **Outdoor escape game** — [Atlantide](https://www.atlantide.io/) app (“Sous surveillance” mission in the Marais)
- **Seine cruise** — [Les Vedettes de Paris](https://www.vedettesdeparis.fr/vdp-cruise/croisiere-en-famille-paris/)

## Walks

- Many [walks](https://parisjetaime.com/a-voir-a-faire-a-paris/balades-a-paris-i038) on the Paris je t’aime site
- [Paris Region shopping neighbourhood by neighbourhood](https://www.visitparisregion.com/fr/parcours/parcours-thematiques/shopping-paris-region-quartier-par-quartier)
