Less than 48 hours to visit one of the iconic cities of the Land of the Rising Sun…

## Activities

- Arrival in Japan at Osaka (Kansai Airport)
- Flights Brussels–Munich and Munich–Osaka
- The Klook app is very handy for buying train tickets
- The Haruka train is decorated with Hello Kitty
- One Day Pass site: [https://oneday-pass.kyoto/?lang=en](https://oneday-pass.kyoto/?lang=en)
- Dropping bags at [Hotel Rings Kyoto](https://hotel-rings.com/en/)
