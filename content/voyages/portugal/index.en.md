18-day itinerary:
Day 1: Guimarães - departure Brussels Charleroi, Porto airport, UNESCO historic centre, tortas de Guimarães, sunset from infinity pool
Day 2: Peneda-Gerês National Park & Ponte de Lima - swimming Ribeiro de Carcerelha, Poço Negro, bridge over Rio Lima
Day 3: Porto
Day 4: Trilho dos Poços Verdes - 7 Lagoas, dinner Guimarães
Day 5: Bom Jesus do Monte
Day 6: Amarante & Douro Valley - visit Amarante, vineyards, Magnifico Douro cruise from Pinhão
Day 7: Aveiro & Costa Nova - visit Aveiro, Porta do cafe
Day 8: Fraga de la Pena
Day 9: Buçaco National Park & Coimbra
Day 10: Monastery of Batalha, Nazaré & Peniche
Day 11: Óbidos - medieval town, ramparts, arrival Mafra
Day 12: Sintra - Palácio da Pena hike, Castelo dos Mouros, Quinta da Regaleira
Day 13: Lisbon - Pastéis de Belém, Jerónimos Monastery, Belém Tower
Day 14: Rota Vincentina - hike from Odemira
Day 15: Praia Do Queimado, Praia Do Sissal, dinner Porto Covo
Day 16: Almograve, Beja, Cape Sardão lighthouse
Day 17: Sagres - Praia de Odeceixe, lighthouse, surf beach
Day 18: Return - São Gonçalo de Lagos, Ponte da Piedade
